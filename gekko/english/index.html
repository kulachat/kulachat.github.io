GEKKO

Gekko Industries Co., Ltd. was setup with the aim to introduce polyurethane pre-insulated pipe (Gekko Pre-insulated Pipe) into the construction, process plant industries in the region.

Gekko Pre-insulated Pipe has a range of both aboveground and underground factory insulated piping system manufactured under stringent quality control condition.
