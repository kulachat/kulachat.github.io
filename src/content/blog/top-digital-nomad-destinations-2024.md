## What to Consider When Choosing

### Internet Reliability
Always research actual internet speeds and reliability, not just advertised speeds. Check nomad forums and communities for real user experiences.

### Community
A strong nomad community can make or break your experience. Look for cities with:
- Multiple co-working spaces
- Regular nomad meetups
- Online community groups
- Established infrastructure for remote workers

### Visa Requirements
Consider how long you can stay legally and what the visa process involves:
- **Tourist visas:** Usually 30-90 days
- **Digital nomad visas:** 6 months to 1 year
- **Working holiday visas:** Up to 1-2 years for eligible countries

### Time Zones
Ensure the location aligns reasonably well with your clients' or team's working hours.

### Healthcare
Research the quality and accessibility of healthcare, and ensure you have appropriate travel insurance.

The best destination for you depends on your:

- **Lifestyle preferences:** Urban vs. nature, nightlife vs. quiet, adventure vs. comfort
- **Personal goals:** Language learning, skill development, networking
